Whales are absolutely magnificient creatures!
whales, specifically the Physeter macrocephalus, can weigh up to 15 tons (females) to 45 tons (males); be 40 feet (females) to 52 feet (males) long; live up to 60 years; and, can be found in Alaska, New England / Mid-Atlantic, Pacific Islands, Southeast, and the West Coast.

These whales are currently threatend by climate change, entanglement in fishing gear, marine debris, ocean noise, oil spills and contaminants, and vessel strikes.
